# Aryan Jha — AI Research & Engineering Portfolio

Building reliable AI systems under uncertainty.

---

## Overview

This repository contains my personal portfolio website, designed to showcase my work at the intersection of:

* AI research (epistemics, evaluation, reliability)
* applied machine learning systems
* full-stack engineering for intelligent products

The goal is not just to present projects, but to demonstrate how I think about building **robust, interpretable, and real-world AI systems**.

---

## Core Focus Areas

### 1. LLM Reliability & Decision Systems

Exploring how large language models behave under uncertainty and how to build structured pipelines that improve:

* consistency
* confidence calibration
* decision traceability

---

### 2. Applied ML Systems

Designing end-to-end systems that move beyond models into usable products:

* Deepfake detection pipelines
* Synthetic hyperspectral data generation
* AI-powered financial assistants

---

### 3. Systems Thinking in AI

Focusing on the layer most portfolios ignore:

* evaluation frameworks
* failure modes
* reliability under distribution shifts
* real-world deployment constraints

---

## Featured Work

### LLM Decision Pipeline Reliability

A structured approach to improving LLM outputs through:

* staged reasoning
* verification layers
* decision constraints

Focus: making LLM outputs **auditable and reliable**, not just fluent.

---

### Deepfake Detection System

A multi-stage detection pipeline combining:

* spatial feature extraction
* temporal inconsistencies
* model-based classification

Focus: robustness against evolving manipulation techniques.

---

### Synthetic Hyperspectral Data Generation

Generating high-dimensional spectral data using deep learning techniques.

Focus: enabling ML in domains with **limited real-world data availability**.

---

### Centsible — AI Financial Assistant

A system designed to reduce impulsive spending and improve savings behavior.

Focus:

* behavioral nudging
* intelligent tracking
* real-world usability

---

### JimBro — Fitness System

A structured fitness tracking and progression platform.

Focus:

* user behavior
* system simplicity
* long-term engagement
